Methods for Phylogeny Reconstruction

Here is the main methods for constructing the Phylogeny Tree, suppose we have the pairwise distances as input from the Alignment-free algorithms.

Distance-matrix methods

UPGMA: Unweighted Pair Group Method with Arithmetic mean

\[ \frac{1}{|A|*|B|}\sum_{x \in A}\sum_{y \in B}d(x,y) \] \[ d(A \cup B),X = \frac{|A|*d_{A,X} + |B|*d_{B,X}}{|A| + |B|} \]

WPGMA: Weighted Pair Group Method with Arithmetic mean

\[ d_{(i \in j),k} = \frac{d_{i,k} + d_{j,k}}{2} \]
